I'm a graphic designer specialising in brand identity, visual systems, and packaging. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous craft and genuine curiosity — informed by designers like Sagmeister, Pentagram, and COLLINS, and grounded in the everyday reality of making things that actually work.

Every project starts with one question: what does this brand need to be? Not just look like — but actually be. The visual language follows from the answer, never the other way round.

/ How I Work

The
process

01

Discovery & Strategy

We start with conversations about the brand, its audience, its competition, and what success actually looks like. No brief, no design — ever.

02

Concept Development

Multiple directions explored in sketch and vector. I think in systems, not single marks — so concepts arrive with context and example applications.

03

Refinement & Craft

The chosen direction is built with precision. Every pixel, curve, and colour decision is intentional, tested across real contexts, and documented.

04

Delivery & Ongoing Support

Full file handoff with guidelines you can actually use. I stay available through launch and beyond for implementation questions or brand evolution.
